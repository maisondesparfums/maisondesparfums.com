The private gardens at La Maison des Parfums are for your sole use. The Century old trees, the tennis court, the river at the far end of the grounds are a dream for nature lovers.

Everything is possible at La Maison des Parfums: wedding receptions, private functions, weekend with friends, or simply a quiet afternoon in the shade.

On site and at your disposal: Tennis, swings, BBQ, deckchairs, row boat and fishing on the river.
