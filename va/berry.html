« Le Berry ?
C’est un moutonnement magique »

Carla Bruni

Come discover Lignières and the Berry, one of France’s most historically wealthy regions.La Route Jacques Coeur and its castles, the medieval city of Bourges, the castles of Ainay le Vieil or Culan, the Orsan and Drulon gardens, Noirlac abbaye, the hedges and forests. Everything is close to La Maison des Parfums

Enjoy music festivals at Lignières’ dynamic concert hall, les “Bains Douches”. Attend horse-races at the local state of the art race-course, go gliding in the nearby village of Fay, cycling on country roads and visiting the vineyards of Quincy, Sancerre and Chateaumeillant. These are just a handful of activities you can indulge in during your stay.
